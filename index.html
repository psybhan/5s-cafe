About 5S Cafe

5S is a Toronto-based cafe founded by Sahar, with over four years of experience in the industry. Together with her son, Sobhan—a seasoned barista and drink specialist—they bring a passion for artisanal beverages, food, and a welcoming atmosphere that feels like home.

Our unique menu features specialty coffee drinks, fresh pastries, baked goods, customized birthday cakes, coffee beans, homemade products, arts for retail, and event hosting. Operating from 7 am to 7 pm, we’re located in a prime spot in the heart of Toronto.
